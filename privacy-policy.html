Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026  ·  Effective date: June 7, 2026

We built Cozy Wellness to help you build healthy habits — not to collect or sell your data. This policy explains clearly what we collect, why, and how you can control it.

1Who We Are

Cozy Wellness (the "App") is operated by Cozy Wellness ("we", "us", or "our"). If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at the contact details at the bottom of this page.

2Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the App's features:

  • Account information: Your email address and password (stored securely via Supabase Auth). You may also provide a display name and avatar during onboarding.
  • Wellness data: Habit check-ins, wellness goals you set, streak counts, and Wellness Points earned — all stored on our servers to sync your progress across devices.
  • Game data: Pet state, garden/farm progress, and cosmetic items you own or purchase.
  • Purchase records: If you make an in-app purchase, we receive a transaction receipt from Apple or Google to verify and fulfill your purchase. We do not receive or store your credit card details.
  • Technical data: App crash reports and basic usage analytics (session start, feature usage counts) to help us fix bugs and improve the App.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any health data from your device's Health app.

3How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To sync your habits, garden, and pet progress across devices
  • To process and fulfill in-app purchases
  • To send you optional push notifications (habit reminders) — only if you grant permission
  • To fix bugs and improve the App
  • To comply with our legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third-party marketers.

4Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase servers, which are hosted on AWS infrastructure. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We enforce Row-Level Security so that users can only access their own data.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong, unique password for your account.

5Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — backend database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Apple App Store — iOS app distribution and in-app purchases (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play — Android app distribution and in-app purchases (Privacy Policy)
  • Expo / EAS — app build and over-the-air updates (Privacy Policy)

6Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data directly within the App (Settings → Delete Account), or by contacting us
  • Portability: Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection / Restriction: Object to or request restriction of certain processing

To exercise any of these rights, use the in-app deletion flow or email us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

8Push Notifications

We may send push notifications for habit reminders and in-game events. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in your device's Settings app. We will always ask for your permission before sending notifications.

9Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law (e.g., purchase transaction records for tax/legal purposes, retained for up to 7 years).

10International Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with applicable law.

11Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via an in-app notification or a prominent notice in the App before the changes take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
